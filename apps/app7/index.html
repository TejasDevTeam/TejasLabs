Find Your Flow. Master Your Breath. Track Your Progress. Yoga Rhythm is your ultimate wellness companion for both beginners starting their holistic journey and advanced practitioners perfecting their sequences. Whether you want to energise your morning with Suryanamaskar, find deep calm through Pranayama, or build your own custom workout flows, Yoga Rhythm gives you the tools to practice perfectly at your own pace.

Features

🙏

Suryanamaskar & Pranayama

A complete yoga library in your hands. Guided step-by-step Suryanamaskar sequences and 14 powerful breathing techniques — including Box Breathing, Anulom Vilom, Bhramari, and the calming 4-7-8 method.

⏱️

Smart Visual Yoga Timer

Stay in the flow, not the clock. The interactive visual timer counts down each pose and breath cycle. Customise duration per pose, adjust breathing speed, and follow hands-free audio cues seamlessly.

🔄

Custom Routines & Yog Nidra

Build your perfect practice, your way. Combine Suryanamaskar, Pranayama, and custom activities into a saved routine. Includes a dedicated Yog Nidra sleep meditation module with a gentle sleep timer.

📅

Progress Tracking & Streaks

Your yoga journey, made visible. A heatmap calendar shows your consistency at a glance. Track daily and longest streaks, log all past sessions, and view stats on total movement and breathing time.
